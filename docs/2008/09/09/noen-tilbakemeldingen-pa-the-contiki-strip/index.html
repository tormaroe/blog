the contiki strip sammenlignes med Dilbert

tirsdag 9. september 2008 the contiki strip

For de som ikke vet det - the contiki strip's visuelle stil er inspirert av en kul tegneserie som heter Order of the Stick (OotS). Det er et svært community knyttet til OotS, inkludert mange fans som lager sine egne tegneserier - sånn som meg.

I går registrerte jeg meg på Giant in the Playground Forums, hvor OotS-gjengen holder til, og annonserte the contiki strip. Og jeg har allerede fått masse bra tilbakemeldinger.., skikkelig gøy altså! Her er et eksempel:

"Nice. It's like Dilbert and OotS, mashed up together, but with a different sort of twist. Dilbert is more about stupid coworkers and programs, and yours is more about realistic but still ridiculous events that happen during programming/work. Keep it up, man! Great job; it seems like you've had several OOTS-style artists look over it, in addition to scriptwriting. It's amazing to think you did all this at once before posting it. Again, keep it up!"

Wow, thanks man!
